## Part 1 — Product Knowledge (background for you)

### 1.1 Canvas & material lines

These are the main materials Louis Vuitton (LV) uses. **Important fact:** "Monogram", "Damier", and "Eclipse" are **coated canvas** (cotton canvas covered with PVC), **not leather**. They are water-resistant and easy to clean. The others below are real leather.

| Line | What it looks like | Key facts |
|---|---|---|
| **Monogram** | Brown canvas with the LV initials, flowers, and stars (quatrefoils) | The most famous LV pattern. Coated canvas. Trimmed with natural (Vachetta) leather. Water-resistant. |
| **Monogram Empreinte** | Soft leather with the Monogram pattern pressed (embossed) into it | Real **full-grain calfskin**, heat-embossed. Soft and supple. More discreet than canvas. |
| **Damier Ebene** | Dark brown and lighter brown checkerboard squares | Coated canvas. Very low-maintenance (usually no Vachetta trim). "Damier" = "checkered" in French. |
| **Damier Azur** | Light cream (ivory) and blue checkerboard squares | Coated canvas. Introduced 2006. Light/summer look. Light color can pick up color transfer, so handle with care. |
| **Epi leather** | Textured leather with fine horizontal grain lines | Real cowhide, vegetable-tanned. Durable, scratch-resistant, water-resistant. Comes in many colors. |
| **Taurillon** | Soft, grained calf/cowhide, plain (no logo) | Real leather. Understated, minimalist. Soft to the touch. |
| **Vachetta leather** | Pale, natural, untreated leather (handles & trim) | Untreated vegetable-tanned cowhide. Scratches easily and darkens to a honey/amber **patina** over time. |
| **Mahina** | Soft leather with the Monogram pattern punched as tiny holes (perforated) | Supple perforated calf leather. The logo appears only on close look. |
| **Monogram Eclipse** | Black and grey Monogram | Coated canvas. Launched 2016. Darker, more masculine look (popular for men). |

### 1.2 Iconic bags

| Bag | Short description |
|---|---|
| **Speedy** | The classic everyday handbag. Soft "doctor-bag" dome shape, rounded handles, zip top, small padlock. Born 1930 (first called the "Express"). Famous sizes: Speedy 25/30/35. |
| **Neverfull** | Open-top tote with thin handles and side laces to change the shape. Comes with a small removable zipped pochette. Very spacious. Introduced 2007. |
| **Keepall** | The classic soft travel/weekend duffle bag. Sizes 45–60 cm. The "Bandoulière" version has a removable shoulder strap. Born 1930. |
| **Alma** | Structured handbag with a dome shape and double zip. Art Deco inspired, from the 1930s. Comes in canvas, Epi, and more. |
| **Capucines** | High-end, quiet-luxury top-handle bag in premium leather. The LV initials are partly hidden under a flap. Introduced 2013. |
| **OnTheGo** | Large structured tote with two strap lengths (hand or shoulder). Great for work and travel. |
| **Pochette Métis** | Compact crossbody bag with an S-lock closure and three inside compartments. |
| **Petite Malle** | A mini trunk-shaped bag with hard corners and an S-lock. Inspired by LV's historic trunks. Released 2014. |
| **Noé** | A drawstring bucket bag. Originally made in the 1930s to carry champagne bottles. |
| **Twist** | A shoulder bag with a sculptural "LV" turn-lock that twists to open. Debuted 2015. |
| **Dauphine** | A retro-modern bag with a bold front LV clasp. Inspired by 1970s designs. |
| **Multi Pochette (Accessoires)** | A hybrid crossbody that combines a pochette, a mini pochette, and a round coin purse. Has a chain and a wide jacquard strap. Monogram canvas or Empreinte leather. |

### 1.3 Product categories

Louis Vuitton sells much more than bags:

- **Handbags** — shoulder bags, totes, crossbody bags, top-handle bags.
- **Small leather goods (SLG)** — wallets, card holders, coin purses, key pouches.
- **Travel & luggage** — trunks, suitcases, duffle bags, travel accessories.
- **Ready-to-wear (RTW)** — women's and men's clothing.
- **Shoes** — sneakers, heels, boots, loafers.
- **Belts** — for women and men.
- **Accessories** — scarves, sunglasses, hats, gloves.
- **Fine jewelry & watches** — rings, necklaces, high-end watches.
- **Fragrance / perfume** — for women and men.
- **Gifts** — small gift items and personalized pieces.

### 1.4 Services & care

- **Hot stamping** — free service to print initials (your name letters) onto small leather goods, luggage tags, and some soft luggage. Can be done in-store, online, or by phone.
- **Made-to-order / personalization** — "My LV Heritage": add initials and stripes with many color choices. Hand-painting is offered only for hard-sided Monogram/Damier luggage.
- **Repairs** — LV has a global network of repair ateliers (workshops) with skilled artisans. Some small repairs are done in-store; bigger repairs go to an atelier. Request via the LV app, in-store, or Client Services.
- **Leather care** — coated canvas can be cleaned with a damp cloth and mild soap. Vachetta (natural leather) is delicate; protect it from water and color transfer.
- **Tax-free shopping** — many tourists can claim a VAT refund. (Confirm the current local rules in Istanbul before promising this.)
- **Gift wrapping** — products come in the signature LV box and dust bag; gift packaging is available.

## Part 2 — Product Vocabulary Bank (40–50 terms)

Format: **English term** — simple definition — *Türkçe*

### A. Materials & patterns

1. **canvas** — a strong woven cloth used to make bags. — *kanvas / branda kumaş*
2. **coated canvas** — canvas covered with a thin plastic layer; water-resistant; not leather. — *kaplamalı kanvas*
3. **leather** — material made from animal skin. — *deri*
4. **full-grain leather** — the best, top layer of leather; very strong. — *tam tabaka deri*
5. **calfskin** — soft leather from a young cow. — *dana derisi*
6. **cowhide** — leather from a cow. — *inek derisi*
7. **Monogram** — the famous LV pattern with letters and flowers. — *Monogram deseni*
8. **Damier** — the checkerboard (squares) pattern. — *dama/kareli desen*
9. **embossed** — a pattern pressed into the leather. — *kabartmalı / baskılı*
10. **perforated** — leather with small holes in a pattern. — *delikli / zımbalı*
11. **grain** — the natural texture/lines on leather. — *deri dokusu*
12. **patina** — the natural color change on leather over time. — *zamanla oluşan renk/patina*
13. **suede** — soft leather with a fuzzy surface. — *süet*

### B. Bag parts & features

14. **handle** — the part you hold to carry the bag. — *sap / kulp*
15. **strap** — the long band to carry on the shoulder or across the body. — *askı / kayış*
16. **adjustable strap** — a strap you can make longer or shorter. — *ayarlanabilir askı*
17. **lining** — the fabric inside the bag. — *iç astar*
18. **hardware** — the metal parts (zip, lock, rings). — *metal aksesuarlar*
19. **zip / zipper** — the closure with a slider and teeth. — *fermuar*
20. **closure** — how the bag closes (zip, lock, magnet). — *kapatma sistemi*
21. **lock / padlock** — a small metal lock on the bag. — *kilit / asma kilit*
22. **compartment** — a separate space inside the bag. — *bölme*
23. **pocket** — a small space to keep things. — *cep*
24. **stitching** — the line of thread that holds the bag together. — *dikiş*
25. **trim** — the leather edges and details on a bag. — *kenar derisi / pervaz*
26. **dust bag** — the soft cloth bag that protects the product. — *toz torbası*

### C. Product types

27. **handbag** — a bag carried by hand or on the shoulder. — *el çantası*
28. **tote** — a large open bag (like the Neverfull). — *büyük çanta / tote*
29. **crossbody bag** — a bag worn across the body. — *çapraz çanta*
30. **clutch** — a small bag without a handle, held in the hand. — *el portföyü / clutch*
31. **wallet** — for money and cards. — *cüzdan*
32. **card holder** — a small case for cards. — *kartlık*
33. **coin purse** — a small purse for coins. — *bozuk para cüzdanı*
34. **small leather goods** — small items like wallets and card holders. — *küçük deri ürünler*
35. **luggage** — bags and cases for travel. — *bavul / valiz*
36. **trunk** — a hard, box-shaped travel case. — *sandık / trunk*
37. **ready-to-wear** — clothing you can buy and wear (not custom). — *hazır giyim*
38. **belt** — worn around the waist. — *kemer*
39. **scarf** — worn around the neck. — *fular / atkı*
40. **sunglasses** — glasses for the sun. — *güneş gözlüğü*
41. **fragrance / perfume** — a nice smell you wear. — *parfüm*

### D. Service & shop words

42. **hot stamping** — printing your initials on leather (free service). — *baş harf baskısı (sıcak baskı)*
43. **personalize / personalization** — make a product your own with initials/colors. — *kişiselleştirme*
44. **made-to-order** — made specially for the client. — *sipariş üzerine yapım*
45. **repair** — to fix a damaged product. — *tamir / onarım*
46. **atelier** — the workshop where artisans make/repair items. — *atölye*
47. **artisan / craftsman** — a skilled person who makes products by hand. — *zanaatkâr / usta*
48. **gift wrapping** — wrapping a product as a present. — *hediye paketi*
49. **tax-free / VAT refund** — money back on tax for tourists. — *vergi iadesi*
50. **waterproof / water-resistant** — does not let water in / resists water. — *su geçirmez / suya dayanıklı*
51. **authentic / genuine** — real, not fake. — *orijinal / hakiki*

## Part 3 — Describing a Product to a Client (A2 example sentences)

Practice these out loud. Each one is simple English + *Türkçe*.

1. **This bag is made of Monogram coated canvas. It is water-resistant.**
   *Bu çanta Monogram kaplamalı kanvastan yapılmıştır. Suya dayanıklıdır.*

2. **This one is real leather. It is soft and very light.**
   *Bu gerçek deri. Yumuşak ve çok hafif.*

3. **The handles are made of natural leather. The color gets darker over time.**
   *Saplar doğal deridendir. Renk zamanla koyulaşır.*

4. **This is the small size. It is perfect for every day.**
   *Bu küçük boy. Her gün için mükemmel.*

5. **The bag has a long strap, so you can wear it across the body.**
   *Çantanın uzun bir askısı var, böylece çapraz takabilirsiniz.*

6. **Inside, there are three compartments and one pocket.**
   *İçinde üç bölme ve bir cep var.*

7. **This bag comes in black, beige, and red.**
   *Bu çanta siyah, bej ve kırmızı renklerde var.*

8. **The hardware is gold-color. It looks very elegant.**
   *Metal aksesuarlar altın renginde. Çok şık görünüyor.*

9. **This is a tote bag. It is big, so you can put a laptop inside.**
   *Bu bir tote çanta. Büyük, içine dizüstü bilgisayar koyabilirsiniz.*

10. **The strap is adjustable. You can make it longer or shorter.**
    *Askı ayarlanabilir. Daha uzun veya daha kısa yapabilirsiniz.*

11. **This wallet matches the bag. They are the same pattern.**
    *Bu cüzdan çantayla uyumlu. Aynı desende.*

12. **This is one of our most popular bags. It is a classic.**
    *Bu en popüler çantalarımızdan biri. Bir klasik.*

## Part 4 — Common Client Questions & Answers (A2)

Each: **Question** → *Türkçe* → **Model answer** → *Türkçe*

1. **Client: "Is this leather?"** *(Bu deri mi?)*
   **You: "This part is coated canvas, and the handles are real leather."**
   *Bu kısım kaplamalı kanvas, saplar ise gerçek deri.*

2. **Client: "Is it waterproof?"** *(Su geçirmez mi?)*
   **You: "The canvas is water-resistant. But please keep the leather parts dry."**
   *Kanvas suya dayanıklı. Ama lütfen deri kısımları kuru tutun.*

3. **Client: "Can I personalize it?"** *(Kişiselleştirebilir miyim?)*
   **You: "Yes. We can add your initials with our free hot stamping service."**
   *Evet. Ücretsiz baş harf baskısı hizmetimizle baş harflerinizi ekleyebiliriz.*

4. **Client: "Where is it made?"** *(Nerede üretildi?)*
   **You: "It is made in France." (or Spain / USA, depending on the item)**
   *Fransa'da üretildi. (ürüne göre İspanya / ABD)*

5. **Client: "Do you have a bigger size?"** *(Daha büyük boyu var mı?)*
   **You: "Yes, this bag comes in three sizes. Let me show you the large one."**
   *Evet, bu çanta üç boyda var. Size büyük olanı göstereyim.*

6. **Client: "What if it breaks? Can you repair it?"** *(Kırılırsa tamir eder misiniz?)*
   **You: "Yes. We have a repair service. Our artisans can fix it in our atelier."**
   *Evet. Tamir hizmetimiz var. Ustalarımız atölyemizde tamir edebilir.*

7. **Client: "Is this the real one / authentic?"** *(Bu orijinal mi?)*
   **You: "Yes, every bag is authentic and comes with a dust bag and a box."**
   *Evet, her çanta orijinaldir ve toz torbası ile kutuyla gelir.*

8. **Client: "Can I get tax-free / a VAT refund?"** *(Vergi iadesi alabilir miyim?)*
   **You: "Yes, for tourists we can prepare the tax-free form. May I see your passport?"**
   *Evet, turistler için vergi iadesi formunu hazırlayabiliriz. Pasaportunuzu görebilir miyim?*
